🚀 HOW TO USE NEW FEATURES
-------------------------------------------------------------------

1. AI AUTO-CATEGORIZATION
   Step 1: Fill in email and date range
   Step 2: Click "Suggest Categories"
   Step 3: Review AI suggestions
   Step 4: Click "Apply As Rules"

2. AUTO-SYNC SETUP
   Step 1: Set hour (e.g., 21 for 9 PM)
   Step 2: Set minute (e.g., 0)
   Step 3: Enter email
   Step 4: Click "Enable Auto-Sync"
   Step 5: Run: ./scripts/setup-cron.sh
   Step 6: Follow prompts to add cron job

3. CHECK RECONSENT
   Step 1: Enter email
   Step 2: Click "Check Status"
   Step 3: If expired, click "Open Reconsent Page"
   Step 4: Complete bank authorization

4. VIEW TUTORIALS
   Scroll to "Tutorials & Resources" section
   Click any video link or template

5. RESET OPTIONS
   Scroll to "Reset & Clear Data"
   Choose specific reset or "Reset Everything"
